This is our minesweeper capstone done by Aram Leblebjian & Joseph Alakach.

To re-train a new model, run the Train.ipynb

To see our AI play the game, install python, keras, tensorflow on your device. Then, go to the directory where you have our model and the Minesweeper_play.py file, and run on cmd: python Minesweeper_play.py
